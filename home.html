HELLO WE ARE SHEANHUB...

Sheane Hub is a community-based organization with the mission of reducing women and youth poverty in rural landscapes through improved monetization of indigenous resources and creating career paths for youths in development.

Learn More

Who We Are

With the rising need for diversified, sustainable solutions for rural life interventions in poverty prevention and reduction, Sheane is committed to developing winged programs that target its main audience, utilizing available and aided resources in Northern Ghana, expanding the market to Rwanda and building impact:

Local Producers Common-Village

A local hub for producer-consumer interaction, production field tourism & sales increments.

Youth Career Starter Programs

Entry level internships, company placements, and career coaching.

Community Social Aid Programs

Child education, health & feeding.

Practical Industry

Formulating nature-based products from indigenous resources (scales markets).

What We Do

We utilize improved monetization systems to increase income rates for local resource producers (workers) while empowering the next generation of skill adopters (YOUTH).

We accelerate innovative monetization of local Indigenous resources produced by the people for the globe (with climate & nature). With nature, we support through action and sensitization. Saving a tree at a time.

We expand inter-regional people reach through sectored organization project collaborations and execution partnerships. Accepting in-kind supports and expert programs execution.

Our Vision

Leaving no community unaided, building economic-rush for indigenous raw materials in Africa, and raising half a million youth in healthy career paths by 2034.
